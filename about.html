Our Story

Stories help us to give meaning to the things around us. This is ours.

The beginning

Hunter Kichen Designs was founded in 2022 on the basis of a belief that everyone is different – and should be treated as such. We are devoted to instill this in everything we do. A family business, born in the South East of London, Hunter Kitchen Designs was created and inspired to do incredible things, for incredible people. With a Kesseler was founded back in 2009 on the basis of a belief that everyone is different – and should be treated as such. We passionately advocate this in everything we do. A family business, born in the heart of Nottinghamshire, Kesseler was created and inspired to do great things, for great clients. With a beautiful bespooke design aesthetic and that unmistakable English spirit, we are here for you.

Our Process (icon)

Every person is different, with contrasting tastes. All houses are dissimilar, just as the people inside those house have their own ideas. Inspirations and passions are unique, and we intend to bring those unique passions to life. The personal tastes and styles of each person are one of a kind, so why shouldn’t their kitchen be? Each kitchen adopts the Hunter methodology and the trained, experienced designers truly understand the value of helping you create the individual kitchen of your dreams.

The Hunter Difference (icon)

Our brand is built on the idea that every kitchen should have a wonderful foundation. Our kitchens are precision-crafted utilising the finest materials and using only the best joining methods. As architects and joiners are the strongest way of building kitchen furniture, we value the craft and only have the best. Our kitchens are structurally more robust than other ‘cam & dowel’ or ‘glue & dowel’ kitchens and will last a lifetime. Pair that with our wide range of modern and traditonal styles and virtually unlimited finishing combinations, we enable our clients to really craft a kitchen unique to them. Something of distinguished superiority. Something exclusive. Something unique. This is what we stand for.
