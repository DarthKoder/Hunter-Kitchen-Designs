We Are Here For You

We care about your individuality.


Our designers specialise in delivering exquisite handmade kitchens to elevate your space. We have been creating beautiful, functional bespoke kitchens for individuals for years. From the British cabinet making methods to the understanding of how to combine different timber finishes with paint colours, you can be confident that the Hunter Design team have the knowledge to design you a luxury, bespoke kitchen.


Discover the very best in modern and traditional design with Hunter Kitchen Design.

Why Hunter Kitchens?

Working with a Hunter designer, you’ll begin the journey of the bespoke kitchen design process. Your designer will work alongside your architects, builders and more (or help source them if required) and design the kitchen you’ve always dreamed of. Working with you and your family, we’ll understand what you truly desire from your kitchen or open-plan living space.


We’ll get to know you deeply, understanding how you’ll use the space and how it’ll be a part of your every day. This helps us to create the best design, pair the perfect appliances and bring together the most amazing décors, colours, worktops and more.


When your eyes rest on a custom Hunter kitchen, they follow the beautiful lines and the ideal proportions of the beautiful cabinetry. Quality British craftsmanship and cabinet-building perfection can be found resonating in the tactile feel of the edging, the soft-closing of every drawer and door, the reassuring weight of each piece of beautiful furniture.
